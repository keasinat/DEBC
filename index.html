FinTech refers to the technology and innovation in the financial sector used to enhance the use, delivery, and access to financial services broadly and inclusively (RGC, 2021).
This workshop is expected to provide an opportunity for the Cambodian FinTech policy development working group to gather input from different stakeholders in the financial sector and FinTech industry to draft an effective and inclusive policy.
